About Us

Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum was founded in 2007 by Nafa Mohamed with the goal of charity. It was established in response to the growing need for support and integration within the Slovakian community in South Manchester. Many Slovakian immigrants and their families face challenges in accessing essential services such as education, healthcare, and legal assistance due to language barriers, cultural differences, and limited resources. The charity aims to provide a supportive environment, helping individuals and families overcome these obstacles, while fostering a sense of community and belonging. By offering practical assistance and promoting cultural understanding, the charity strives to create a stronger, more inclusive society for everyone. .

Our team consists of dedicated volunteers and staff who are passionate about making a difference in Slovakia. We work closely with local communities and partners to ensure that our programs are effective and sustainable.
